Your Analysis of Zebrafish protein expression

YAZ is a tool for visualisation of protein expression data acquired from NIST Library of Peptide Tandem Mass Spectra for Zebrafish (Danio rerio) and Carp (Cyprinus carpio). To get the expression map you should simply enter the UniProt ID of the protein of interest and choose the colour. For five tissues information for both genders is available. You can see the difference in a bar-plot under the expression map and choose whether to take mean of values for males and females or to discard males.
