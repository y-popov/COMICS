COMICS is a tool for visualisation of protein expression data acquired from NIST Library of Peptide Tandem Mass Spectra for model organism (currently Zebrafish (Danio rerio) and Carp (Cyprinus carpio) are available). To get the expression map you should upload your dataset (or select one from predefined datasets), then simply enter the UniProt ID of the gene/protein of interest and specify some parameters of picture.
